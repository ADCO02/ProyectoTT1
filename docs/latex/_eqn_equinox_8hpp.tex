\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Computes the equation of the equinoxes from nutation and obliquity values. 

This function calculates the equation of the equinoxes, defined as the product of the nutation in longitude and the cosine of the mean obliquity. It represents the difference between apparent and mean sidereal time, or equivalently, the right ascension of the mean equinox referred to the true equator and equinox.

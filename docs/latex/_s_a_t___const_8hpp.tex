\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Definition of astronomical and mathematical constants used in the library. 

This header defines key physical constants such as the speed of light, planetary gravitational parameters, Earth parameters, and unit conversion constants.

\begin{DoxyNote}{Note}
Constants are based on DE430 ephemeris and WGS-\/84. 
\end{DoxyNote}

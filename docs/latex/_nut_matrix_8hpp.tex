\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Computes the nutation matrix from mean to true equator and equinox. 

Constructs the transformation matrix that accounts for the effects of nutation, converting coordinates from the mean equator and equinox of date to the true equator and equinox. This transformation is essential for high-\/precision astronomical calculations, as it includes periodic variations caused by the gravitational influence of the Moon and Sun on Earth\textquotesingle{}s equatorial bulge.

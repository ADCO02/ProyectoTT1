\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Performs the Gibbs method of orbit determination to compute velocity. 

This function implements the Gibbs method to determine the velocity vector at the middle of three given position vectors in space. It uses vector operations to check coplanarity and consistency of the orbit and returns the computed velocity along with angles between vectors and an error status.

\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
Computes the acceleration due to the harmonic gravity field of the central body. 

This function calculates the gravitational acceleration exerted on a satellite by the Earth\textquotesingle{}s harmonic gravity field up to specified degree and order. It transforms the satellite position into the body-\/fixed frame, evaluates the spherical harmonic terms using associated Legendre polynomials, and returns the acceleration vector in the inertial frame.
